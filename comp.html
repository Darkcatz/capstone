How do I become a person of character?


What are values?

Values are the degree of importance of something that you do.

Why is it important?

They are important because it is what you think is more valuable than another thing and it will help you have better or worse ethics.

How do I develop them on my own?

You can first list from the most important to least important things that you do. Then you can ask yourself questions if you are valuing the right thing.

Why do people bully others?

People bully others because of their own insecurities and stress and they want to take it out on another weaker individual.

What is bullying?

Bullying is when a person is harming another person physically or psychologically.

Who plays a role in it?

In bullying, the bystanders play a big role. This is because that they are not doing anything and it shows the victim that no one cares about him or her.

How do we stop it?

The way to stop bullying is to show the bully that what he is doing is not ok. We should not use physical measures but to tell an adult or not be bystanders.

Why is compassion important?

Compassion is very important because it shows a person that you care about them and this can help them a lot in life.

What is Compassion?

Compassion is when you show sympathy or empathy towards a person.

How can I demonstrate it as part of my character?

We can show that we care about others instead of being rude and not listening to them. We should show that we care about them and help them when they need it.
