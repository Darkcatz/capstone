What is self- esteem and self-efficacy?

Self-esteem is a person’s beliefs about their own worth and value. It is important to have high self-esteem due to it influencing many people’s choices and decisions. By having high self-esteem are motivated to take care of themselves and try to be successful in their own goals. Self-efficacy is the belief to be able to do a certain task in their abilities. The more you do a task it will make you more confident in being able to complete that task. By having people who encourage you it will make you feel stronger about your self-efficacy.


What are feelings?

Every person on this planet has feelings which are a part of us since we were born. Feelings are emotions that people have based on something that has happened in their environment.

Why do I have them?

We have emotions due to chemicals being released out of our brains to signal what emotion we should feel. When we have these emotions it makes us be able to relate or understand others.

Why do I get angry, sad, etc?

The majority of people get feelings from when someone or something in their environment affects them or someone they care about. So, if someone broke your TV you would get angry due to that being very important to you. You would get the feeling of being angry by you having an attachment to that item.

Why is goal setting important?

A goal is planning you do for the future. Goals help us to prepare for the future by having us plan out how we are going to tackle certain tasks. This allows us to have enough time to put a good amount of effort into our project. These goals will make you be successful in life where ever you decide to go.

Why we should set goals.

  • Helps to give you something to focus on in life. It helps to give you something on your mind to focus to give you something to look forward too. Without a goal, we have nothing to strive to become or do. By not having any goal to show your talents or abilities while becoming useless and you will have an unsuccessful life.
  • Show how to measure your progress in life. This will allow you to have a benchmark to compare yourself with. Helps you to know how much more or what you have to do to be able to complete your goal.
  • By setting a goal will help you to stop procrastinating over projects or work. It makes you more accountable to have to complete that certain job. The goals will stay in your mind more so that you will remember to complete that certain set of work. This will help you to break up large projects so that they are not as challenging at the end
  • Goals help to give people motivation to complete their tasks. By having motivation helps you to get the momentum in the first place to complete your tasks. It also helps you to put all of your efforts into your work so that it is your best.

How do I problem solve?

When problem solving it is important to have confidence in the process you are taking to solving the issue. By making goals it helps to set goals by having you plan out how you will resolve this problem that you are facing in life. You also need to understand what your problem is and how you are going to solve it. Then you need to question and think how you are going fix or resolve the problem. Then you need to decide how you are going to fix it and do it till it is complete.

The four steps of problem solving:

  1. Define the problem
  2. Generate alternatives
  3. Evaluating and selecting alternatives
  4. Implement solutions

How should I make healthy decisions?

  1. Understand what you are going to decide between. This will help you to develop how you will be able to solve a certain issue.
  2. Be knowledgeable about the topic so that you do not make a false decision. By knowing the situation will help you to understand the types of decisions you can possibly make.
  3. Make sure your decision is based on your values, morals, and ideas. So that it is not a rash decision which you will later regret.
  4. Make sure to look at the positives and negatives of every option so that you can make the best decision possible. Then try to think of the whole outcome of every decision that you are thinking about.
  5. Ask the people you trust to tell you how to resolve the situation. Do to them possibly having experience about about that certain decision.
  6. Finally, choose a decision and make sure you follow your results. Make sure it was the way that you want it to come out as. If not next time you run into this problem you will make the right decision and everyone makes mistakes.
