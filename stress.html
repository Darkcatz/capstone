How do I cope with life?

I can cope with life by managing my time and well and managing my anger. This way I will be upset less often and be able to be happy. I can also be less stressed and that leads towards a healthy and long life.


What is stress?

Stress is when your brain is under emotional strain from certain circumstances. This is most likely caused by a lack of time management.

How do I manage it?

Some ways to manage stress are:

  • Exercise
  • Get more Sleep
  • Talk to a someone that will listen
  • Manage your time well
  • Relax

What is Eustress

Eustress is a type of beneficial stress that is either psychological or physical.
